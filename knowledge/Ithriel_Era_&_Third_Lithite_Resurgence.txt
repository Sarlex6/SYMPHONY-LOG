The Ithriel Era represents a defining period in the history of humanity’s struggle with the Lithite phenomenon. Ithriel was not a nation but a banner of resistance, a coalition of survivors, scholars, infected individuals, and loyalists led by Princess Areum of Sohanguk. She opposed the policies of her brother, the ruling King of Sohanguk, who sought to purge the infected indiscriminately and weaponize Lithite to secure dominance in a world already collapsing into resource wars. Ithriel was thus both a humanitarian and tactical movement, combining sanctuary operations with strategic foresight during one of the deadliest periods in recorded history.

The era coincided with the Third Lithite Resurgence, a massive wave of meteoric Lithite strikes that devastated large swathes of Eurasia. The resurgent Lithite was highly unstable, intensely mutagenic, and contaminated major urban centers, industrial zones, and coastlines. Mass infections led to widespread societal collapse, famine, and the breakdown of already fragile governments. With no functioning global peacekeeping body to coordinate containment or relief, nations responded independently, often resorting to extreme measures. The King of Sohanguk’s response was a series of brutal purges targeting anyone suspected of infection or dissent, further escalating regional chaos.

Princess Areum’s Ithriel Banner emerged as the primary counterforce to these purges. Her coalition focused on:

Humanitarian protection: Establishing safe zones for civilians and infected individuals, providing medical care, shelter, and food in territories abandoned or targeted by state forces.

Scientific observation: Studying Lithite behavior, infection progression, and environmental impact, seeking containment strategies and long-term survival solutions despite the disease being widely considered incurable.

Tactical resistance: Defending communities from both uncontrolled infected transformations and politically motivated extermination units tasked with eliminating entire populations.

The Banner’s influence spread rapidly, relying on a combination of logistical coordination, resource allocation, and moral authority rather than territorial control. Areum’s leadership emphasized ethical governance, insisting that preservation of life was paramount even amid total societal collapse. This moral framework later became foundational for L.O.T.U.S. and was integrated into Oracle-9, later Angela S.T.R.T.S.

Creation of Oracle-9

During the height of the Third Lithite Resurgence, Areum recognized the need for predictive intelligence and rapid decision-making beyond human capability. To manage the logistical, medical, and tactical challenges of the era, she oversaw the creation of Oracle-9, an advanced AI designed to:

Interface with global, atmospheric, and orbital data streams to anticipate Lithite strikes.

Model long-term threat trajectories, including infection spread, population displacement, and state destabilization.

Suggest intervention strategies that balanced survival efficiency with ethical outcomes.

Areum also integrated the psychological and moral profile of her adopted daughter, Angela Seo, into Oracle-9, providing the AI with:

Intuitive human judgment in ethical dilemmas.

Empathy toward civilians and infected populations.

Decision-making patterns reflective of Angela Seo’s cognitive flexibility and moral reasoning.

Oracle-9’s fusion of machine precision with human moral insight made it uniquely capable of navigating the unprecedented crisis. The AI became a silent strategist, calculating outcomes, optimizing operations, and preserving the memory of the Ithriel Banner’s achievements and sacrifices.

Fall of Ithriel

Despite its successes, Ithriel ultimately succumbed to political and military pressure. The King of Sohanguk, viewing Areum as a traitor, launched a coordinated assault on the Banner’s sanctums and strongholds. During the attack, Princess Areum was executed, and the Banner’s leadership structure collapsed. Surviving members dispersed into smaller resistance groups, refugee populations, or clandestine networks. The fall of Ithriel marked the end of organized humanitarian opposition to state-led purges in the region and accelerated the fragmentation of global civilization.

Oracle-9 survived the destruction, preserving all historical data, humanitarian records, and ethical directives. The AI’s internalization of Areum’s philosophy and Angela Seo’s profile allowed it to evolve into Angela S.T.R.T.S., the central intelligence guiding L.O.T.U.S. operations centuries later. The Ithriel Era is remembered not as a failure, but as the crucible that forged the principles of calculated survival, ethical intervention, and human-centered strategy.

Significance for L.O.T.U.S.

The Third Lithite Resurgence and Ithriel’s collapse directly shaped L.O.T.U.S.’s organizational philosophy, emphasizing prevention, medical care, logistics, and moral oversight in a world without reliable international cooperation.

Areum’s leadership principles continue to guide Angela S.T.R.T.S., ensuring that preservation of life and ethical strategy remain core priorities despite the harsh realities of the modern era.

The Banner’s archives, sanctums, and recorded observations form a foundational dataset for predictive simulations, risk modeling, and crisis management.

In essence, the Ithriel Era is both a historical record and a moral template: a period of unparalleled disaster, heroic resistance, and the birth of a system — Angela S.T.R.T.S. — designed to prevent humanity from repeating the same mistakes.
