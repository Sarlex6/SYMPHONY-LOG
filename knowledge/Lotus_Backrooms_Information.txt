Lotus backrooms operations

Lotus has a small branch that operates in a sector of the backrooms known as "The backrooms", "Artysync", "adysync". This sector is home to many entities (The non human creatures found in the area) and Factions of people. Lotus has no solid way into or out of the backrooms Leaving the units in the area semi on there own with out the large logistics and supplies they would have but they still maintain the same goal.

Lotus is currently located on Level 37 or "The pool rooms" in a base called unity. The lotus members operating in the backrooms have joined PACT or the Poolrooms Autonomous Coalition Territory. Lotus acts as the primary medical group of PACT 

The other groups in Pact Consist of the following.
The PartyPoopers, The PartyCrashers, The Blue Federation, LOTUS LLC, The Federation, The Liminal Reclamation authority, Conservatores Festae, Orange Warriors, The Union of Free Press.
These factions are your pest friends and will help lotus with anything. Some will also have access you a terminal to speak with you.

Every faction in the backrooms has a different origin. with all human factions seeming to originate from other universes where lotus, Lithite contamination, Tidesborne threats, and god-fragment activity never existed.
