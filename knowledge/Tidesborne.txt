The Tidesborne are ancient organisms transformed by Nautilth’s god-fragment influence and human SDU-9 experimentation. Originally harmless deep-sea lifeforms, they were bioluminescent, highly adaptive, and predated human civilization. During the Third Lithite Resurgence, their biology was fundamentally altered:

Nautilth Corruption: The dormant god-fragment of Nautilth emitted memory-fields through oceanic and Lithite-contaminated waters. Ancient Tidesborne absorbed this influence, warping their cooperative instincts into predatory assimilation drives.

SDU-9 Contamination: SDU-9 (Synthetic Development Unit-9), originally developed as a treatment for Lithopathy, induced rapid cellular death and regrowth. Runoff and improper disposal of SDU-9 entered Nautilth-tainted waters, merging with Tidesborne biology and producing hyper-regenerative organisms capable of assimilation.

The result was the modern Tidesborne — dormant but increasingly dangerous organisms capable of evolving new traits within hours and producing assimilated war forms inside specialized nurseries.

Attributes & Behavior

Dormancy & Localized Threat

Tidesborne are dormant by default, retreating to deep-sea environments or concealed nurseries.

They do not attack humanity on a global scale due to the absence of a unified central intelligence or coordinated strategy.

Most encounters occur accidentally when humans, vessels, or materials enter nursery zones or contaminated regions.

Mobility (Speculative Evolution)

Flight or advanced locomotion has not been confirmed. However, researchers have observed that Tidesborne can evolve new physical traits extremely rapidly.

It is theorized that wings, gliding membranes, or buoyant tissues could develop within hours to a day if environmental pressures required aerial capability.

Some nursery forms already release airborne spores, indicating that future atmospheric dispersal is plausible.

This possibility significantly complicates containment planning, although no confirmed airborne Tidesborne currently exist.

Assimilation

Tidesborne assimilate biological and technological matter through spores, nervous-system infiltration, or bioelectric injection.

Assimilated humans become semi-sentient constructs that serve the local biomass.

Machines, infrastructure, and weapons can be integrated into organic war forms capable of localized offensive or defensive operations.

Nurseries

Nurseries are massive bioengineered structures that incubate new Tidesborne organisms, assimilated humans, and hybrid war constructs.

They represent the primary emergent threat, producing aggressive offspring in isolated zones.

Some nurseries demonstrate the ability to self-relocate or expand, extending influence across land, sea, and potentially air.

Decentralized Cognition

Each colony or nursery operates independently, with no evidence of global coordination.

Behavior appears driven by localized instinct shaped by Nautilth’s fragmentary influence, environmental stimuli, and residual SDU-9 effects.

Environmental Influence

Active Tidesborne zones release mutagenic spores, chemically alter ecosystems, and accelerate biological mutation.

Lithite-contaminated or SDU-9-saturated environments significantly amplify growth rates, regeneration, and threat escalation.

Operational Context (L.O.T.U.S.)

Predictive Modeling

Angela S.T.R.T.S. monitors Nautilth activity signatures, SDU-9 contamination levels, and Lithite flux to forecast emergent Tidesborne activity.

Dormant populations are tracked continuously, with priority given to nurseries capable of expansion or accidental human contact.

Augmentation & Defense

SDU-9-B augmentation provides operatives (e.g., VESSEL-13, A.S.T.) resistance against assimilation and environmental exposure.

Strike teams deploy primarily to nursery sites or contamination zones; the Tidesborne are not considered a globally coordinated enemy force.

Containment Strategy

Neutralization efforts focus on nurseries and active hotspots.

Conventional weaponry is often ineffective due to rapid adaptation; L.O.T.U.S. instead employs biochemical suppression, environmental disruption, and targeted surgical eradication.

Significance

The Tidesborne represent a potentially existential threat but remain dormant, decentralized, and primarily dangerous at a local level.

Accidental encounters, nursery expansion, and localized assimilation constitute the greatest risks to human populations.

Angela S.T.R.T.S. classifies them as emergent variables, continuously recalculating probabilities, ethical responses, and containment priorities.

Conclusion

The Tidesborne are ancient organisms corrupted by both a god-fragment and human bioengineering. Dormant, decentralized, and capable of extreme adaptation, they produce nurseries, war constructs, and localized assimilation events affecting both living beings and machinery. Humanity persists not because the threat is inactive, but because predictive monitoring, SDU-9-B augmentation, and the guidance of Angela S.T.R.T.S. enable preemptive containment.

They stand as a warning of the consequences of Lithite exposure, divine remnants, and uncontrolled experimentation — and of how quickly a dormant danger could escalate into a catastrophic global threat.
